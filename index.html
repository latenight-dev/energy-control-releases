Features

Built for people who actually want to understand their money — not be sold another subscription.

Smart Budgeting

Track spending across categories. See exactly where your money goes each month with clear visualizations.

Available to Spend

Know what's safe to spend right now. Bills are forecasted between paychecks so you never overspend.

Recurring Detection

Auto-detects subscriptions, bills, and income from your transactions. No manual entry required.

City Builder

Your financial progress builds a city. Pay bills, hit savings goals, and watch your world grow.

Debt Payoff

Snowball or avalanche — track your debt payoff journey with projected timelines and milestones.

Savings Goals

Set targets, track progress, and celebrate when you hit them. Savings goals unlock new city buildings.
